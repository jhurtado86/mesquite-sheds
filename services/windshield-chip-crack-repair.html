Windshield Chip Repair in McAllen — Done Right

If you've driven Expressway 83 or I-2 lately, you already know how it happens. A truck passes, a rock kicks up, and just like that — there's a chip in your windshield. It feels small, so you keep driving. But in South Texas heat, a chip that looks repairable today can spread across your windshield in a few hot afternoons sitting in a parking lot.

Windshield chip repair in McAllen is what our team does best. Caught early, a chip is fixed in about 30 minutes, costs most customers nothing with insurance, and keeps your windshield structurally solid. Waiting — especially in RGV heat — turns a quick $0 fix into a full windshield replacement.

When Can a Chip Be Repaired — and When Do You Need a Replacement?

Here's how to tell the difference — so you know what you're dealing with before you call.

We Can Repair It If…

  • ✓ The chip is smaller than a quarter
  • ✓ Any crack is shorter than 6 inches
  • ✓ The damage isn't directly in your line of sight while driving
  • ✓ The chip hasn't been sitting for weeks collecting debris

Catching it early is the key. In RGV heat, chips spread faster than most people expect — a chip that's repairable today may not be next week.

You'll Need a Replacement If…

  • ✗ The crack is longer than 6 inches and still spreading
  • ✗ The damage sits directly in the driver's line of sight
  • ✗ The chip has branched into multiple cracks
  • ✗ The edge of the windshield is compromised

When replacement is the right call, our technician handles that too — same warranty, same honest pricing, same attention to the work.
